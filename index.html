Daily Health Tips

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 6-8 glasses of water daily. Keep a water bottle nearby as a reminder. If plain water is boring, try adding a slice of lemon or cucumber.

🥗 Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your meals.

🚶‍♂️ Move Every Day

Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity each day.

😴 Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep. Keep your bedroom cool and dark. Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime for better rest.

Common Health Conditions

Diabetes

Manage your blood sugar levels with a balanced diet and regular exercise.

Hypertension

Monitor your blood pressure and reduce salt intake.

Asthma

Keep your inhaler handy and avoid triggers.
