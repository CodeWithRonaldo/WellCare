💡 Health Tips

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 6–8 glasses of water daily. Add lemon or cucumber for flavor.

🥗 Eat Balanced Meals

Include fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains every day.

🚶‍♀️ Stay Active

Exercise 30 minutes daily—walk, dance, or stretch to stay fit.

😴 Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7–8 hours of quality sleep. Create a relaxing bedtime routine.

🧘 Manage Stress

Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or journaling regularly.

🦷 Dental Health

Brush twice daily and floss. Regular dental checkups matter too!
